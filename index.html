Artist Bayar graduated from the School of Fine Arts in 1994, studying painting and design. He is an artist who has been painting for 25 years after graduating from school.

He said, "I am a very calm and meek person. This behavior probably affects the color of the work. Perhaps, it cannot be denied that bright colors dominate the work of a person with a sharp personality.

The artist, who spends 12 to 16 hours a day in his studio, says, "I don't like to depict terrible, dark things."
